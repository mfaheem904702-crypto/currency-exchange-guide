## Why USD to PKR Matters

The value of the Pakistani Rupee against the US Dollar affects imports, exports, fuel prices, inflation, and the overall economy. A stronger dollar often means higher costs for imported goods and services.

## Open Market vs Interbank Rate

There are two major exchange rates in Pakistan:

### Open Market Rate

The open market rate is the price at which currency exchange companies buy and sell US Dollars.

### Interbank Rate

The interbank rate is used by banks for transactions between financial institutions.
